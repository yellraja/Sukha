About Sukha

Sukha, a Sanskrit word meaning "happiness" or "ease," is a student-run mental health club founded at GITAM University with the mission of creating a supportive community that fosters well-being and promotes mental wellness.

Our Mission

Our aim is to provide a safe space for individuals to share their thoughts, feelings, and experiences without fear of judgment. We prioritized creating an environment that encouraged openness, empathy, and understanding.

What We Do

Organize events and workshops on topics such as:

  • Breaking down the stigma around mental health
  • Prioritizing self-care
  • Suicide prevention
  • Managing FOMO (fear of missing out)
  • Create anonymous platforms for individuals to share their thoughts and feelings
  • Host offline events that promoted relaxation and stress relief
  • Provide opportunities for individuals to connect with others and seek support
